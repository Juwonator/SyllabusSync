# SyllabusSync Product Requirements Document

## Vision

SyllabusSync helps Nigerian students prepare for WAEC, NECO, JAMB, GCE, and JUPEB examinations through authentic CBT practice, classroom learning, analytics, and AI-powered support.

## Target Users

- Secondary school students
- WAEC candidates
- NECO candidates
- JAMB candidates
- JUPEB candidates

## Core Features

### CBT Practice

- Objective Questions
- Theory Questions
- Timed Exams

### Classroom

- Notes
- Videos
- Progress Tracking

### Analytics

- Performance Tracking
- Weak Area Detection

### Gamification

- XP
- Streaks
- Achievements

### AI Tutor

- Topic Explanations
- Question Explanations
- Personalized Help
